- Use meaningful english words for variables.
- Supported types: integer, character, and float.
- Avoid using "to".
- for loop Syntax: for "iterator" in range from "start" till "end".
- Use the 'variable' keyword before the variable name to print its value.
- At most 3 operands are allowed in "assign" statement.
- To compile the program, say "compile". It will save and then compile.
- To save the program, say "save".
- To stop listening at any point, say "exit".
- After compiling the program restart the framework.
